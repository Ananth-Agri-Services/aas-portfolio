Services

In India, farmers are challenging major problems like lack of machinery, and all intercultural implements for farming. Through our services, we are providing all the required equipment to the farmers and also reducing marketing and transportation.

JCB's and borewells

Through our Ananth Agri services, farmers can easily avail the JCB & Borewells whenever they are required through a rental basis.

Tractors and harvesters

Not only JCB & borewells, but we also provide the tractors & harvesters to ease the farmer's work.

transporting goods

We provide a variety of vehicles according to the requirement of goods to transport them from one place to another.

Sprayers and water tankers

Easy negotiable services of sprayers & water tankers to help the farmers in every manner.

Fertilizers & chemicals

Farmers can easily avail fertilizers & pesticides to grow their income.

Cereals, vegetables & fruits

Not only machinery, but we provide cereals, pulses , groceries, vegetables & fruits too.
